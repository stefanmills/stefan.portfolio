One question, four angles: when data is noisy — or adversarial — what can we still trust?

Flagship · Targeting ISR / JMIS

Synthetic Noise: How AI-Generated Content Affects the Information Value of Financial Reddit Communities

An ensemble of AI-text detectors — fine-tuned RoBERTa, DetectGPT, GPTZero — with Monte Carlo dropout for uncertainty quantification, applied to millions of records and paired with a difference-in-differences design to measure how synthetic content degrades crowd signals in financial communities.

Mills, S. & Wang, B. — in preparation for a Senior Scholars' Basket journal
